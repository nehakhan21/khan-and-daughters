2010-2015 Crop

Fighting thorugh the California droght, our rice farms remained strong and perservered through a dry patch. Farming with only 80% irrigation, our rice met the market level of moisture: between the 11-15 range within days of harvest.

2015-2019 Crop

In 2015-17, we harvested in early October and placed our rice in our state-of-the-art dryers for 2 weeks before shipping to ADM Milling Company. The following years, we harvested in the end of September, leaving the rice to dry for 3.5 weeks and shipping shortly after.

2020 Crop Predictions

Due to a high volume of seed and water, we predict our 2020 crop will be ready to harvest in late September, with a moisture level of 12-15, drying and ready to ship within the first 2 weeks of October.
